## 📱 日常生活 (Daily Life)

---

### ☀️ #☀-天氣-weather

**Purpose:** Hong Kong weather forecasts and rain alerts.

**What to ask here:** Current weather, forecasts, rain probability, weekend weather planning.

**Example prompts:**
1. `今日天氣點？`
2. `聽日會唔會落雨？`
3. `呢個週末天氣好唔好，適唔適合行山？`

**What you get back:** Temperature, humidity, rain chance, UV index, and a short plain-language forecast. Usually 3–5 lines.

**Automatic:** Weather posts at 8:00 AM HKT daily. Hourly rain alerts post only when rain is likely.

**Status:** ✅ Stable
**For normal users:** Yes — one of the most useful channels.

---

### 📅 #📅-日曆-calendar

**Purpose:** View, add, and manage Google Calendar events.

**What to ask here:** Today's schedule, add meetings, check weekly plans, reschedule events.

**Example prompts:**
1. `今日有咩event？`
2. `加聽日3pm同Peter開會，一個鐘`
3. `呢個星期五有冇空？`

**What you get back:** A list of events with times, or confirmation that an event was created/modified.

**Requires:** Google account connected (already set up for cheafi.chan121@gmail.com).

**Status:** ✅ Stable
**For normal users:** Yes — essential for daily planning.

---

### ⏰ #⏰-提醒-reminders

**Purpose:** Set one-time reminders that ping you at a specific time.

**What to ask here:** Quick reminders — "remind me at X to do Y."

**Example prompts:**
1. `提醒我5pm覆客`
2. `Remind me at 9am tomorrow to call the dentist`
3. `30分鐘後提醒我食藥`

**What you get back:** Confirmation of the reminder. When the time comes, the bot sends a notification message in Discord.

**Note:** These are one-time reminders only. For recurring tasks or todo lists, use Todoist or Calendar instead.

**Status:** ✅ Stable
**For normal users:** Yes — very handy for quick "don't forget" moments.

---

## 💬 一般 (General)

**Purpose:** The main channel. Ask anything here.

**What to ask here:** Literally anything. This is home base. The bot picks the right skill automatically.

**Example prompts:**
1. `幫我plan今日`

## FAQ

**Q: Can I ask anything in any channel?**
A: Yes. Channels suggest a topic but don't limit what the bot can do.

**Q: Do I need all these channels?**
A: No. #一般 handles everything. Other channels are optional shortcuts.

**Q: What language should I use?**
A: Cantonese, English, or a mix — all work naturally.

**Q: Which channels should I actually use?**
A: Start with #一般. Add #☀-天氣-weather and #📅-日曆-calendar if you want automatic topic context.
