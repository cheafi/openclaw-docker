## Core Products

### Autodesk Forma (flagship for prospecting)

- **What:** Cloud-based early-stage design + analysis platform
- **For:** Architects, urban planners, developers doing feasibility studies
- **Key value:** Sun/wind/noise analysis, massing options, regulatory checks — all in ONE platform
- **Speed claim:** 2-3 weeks → 2-3 days for feasibility studies
- **Differentiator:** Real-time environmental analysis during design (competitors do it after)
- **Best for:** Large-scale projects — airports, mixed-use, master planning

### Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC)

- **What:** Connected construction management platform
- **Products:** Build (field mgmt), Takeoff (quantification), BIM Collaborate (design coordination)
- **For:** General contractors, specialty contractors, owners
- **Key value:** Single platform from design through construction, real-time field data
- **Differentiator:** Connects design (Revit/BIM) to field execution seamlessly

### Autodesk Revit

- **What:** BIM authoring tool (architecture, structure, MEP)
- **For:** Design firms, engineering consultancies
- **Key value:** Industry standard for BIM, parametric design, collaboration
- **Note:** Most APAC targets already use Revit — use as conversation starter, not primary sell
